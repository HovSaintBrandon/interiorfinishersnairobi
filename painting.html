Professional Painting & Texturing in Nairobi

Breathe life into your walls with premium decorative coatings, flawless solid colors, and expert wallpaper hanging.

Transform Spaces with High-End Wall Coatings

A professional paint job does far more than change a color; it protects your walls, maximizes ambient light, and establishes the aesthetic foundation of your space. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we provide premium residential and commercial painting services that guarantee uniform coverage, razor-sharp edges, and long-lasting durability.

From luxury textured stucco feature walls in Runda living rooms to heavy-duty weather guard exterior coatings for commercial blocks in Westlands and seamless wallpaper alignments in Kilimani apartments, our team is equipped with the skills and tools to handle any wall finishing challenge.

Our Decorative Finishes & Styles

  • Italian Stucco (Venetian): A polished, marble-like decorative texture applied in multiple thin layers using a trowel, creating a luxurious gloss depth.
  • Silk & Matte Finishes: We apply washable premium acrylic paints that offer a smooth sheen (silk) or a soft texture that hides wall imperfections (matte).
  • Metallic & Texture Skimming: Special effects paints that mimic concrete wash, brushed metal, or sandy dunes to create tactile accent points.
  • Wallpaper Application: Flawless pattern matching and paste application for vinyl, non-woven, and fabric wallpapers without air bubbles or visible seams.

Our Painting & Preparation Protocol

We strictly follow a professional coating workflow to ensure paint doesn't peel or flake under temperature variations:

  1. Surface Preparation: We scrape off old peeling paint, seal any damp cracks with waterproof fillers, and wash off dirt or grease.
  2. Sanding & Wall Skimming: We sand the wall smooth and apply premium skimming plaster to level out uneven concrete surfaces.
  3. Undercoat Priming: We apply a high-grip sealer/undercoat primer to ensure optimal paint adhesion and uniform color distribution.
  4. Double Topcoat Application: We apply two coats of premium paint (Crown Paints or equivalent top brands) using lint-free rollers for a uniform finish.
  5. Clean-up & Inspection: We tape all baseboards and fixtures, and perform a complete clean-up of the site upon completion.

Painting & Texturing Cost in Nairobi

Plaster wall skimming and painting runs between KES 600 to KES 900 per square meter. Premium Italian stucco textured paint installations range from KES 1,200 to KES 2,500 per square meter depending on the texture style and complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What paint finish is best for living rooms vs. bathrooms?

A: For living rooms and bedrooms, a smooth Silk or Matte finish is ideal. For bathrooms and kitchens, we use specialized anti-fungal semi-gloss or eggshell paints that resist humidity and steam.

Q: Why is my wall skimming peeling off, and how do you fix it?

A: Peeling is usually caused by plastering over moisture-ridden walls or failing to use a primer. We scrape the peeling sections, treat the wall for dampness with moisture sealers, and re-skim using external-grade fillers before painting.

Q: Do you supply the paint, or does the client buy it?

A: We offer both options. We can provide a comprehensive quote covering labor, scaffolding, primers, and premium paints, or we can provide a labor-only quote where the client buys their preferred colors.
