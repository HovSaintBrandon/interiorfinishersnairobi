Office Partitioning Solutions in Kenya

Optimizing workspace efficiency with stylish, durable, and functional partitioning systems.

Create Modern, Collaborative & Private Office Layouts

A well-planned office layout is the cornerstone of corporate productivity and employee satisfaction. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we design and install premium office partitioning systems that maximize floor efficiency, control sound transmission, and elevate your corporate brand image.

From glass-enclosed executive boardrooms in Westlands to soundproof drywall cubicles in Upperhill and open-plan team desks in the Nairobi CBD, we specialize in high-end office fitouts tailored to your operational needs. Our layouts strike the perfect balance between open collaboration and acoustic privacy.

Our Partitioning Systems

  • Frameless Glass Partitions: Utilizes heavy-duty tempered safety glass (10mm or 12mm) secured in minimalist perimeter tracks. Ideal for creating light-filled spaces while retaining sound dampening.
  • Aluminium Profile Partitions: A versatile and cost-effective framework featuring high-grade powder-coated aluminium frames with choice of glass, MDF boards, or gypsum boards.
  • Acoustic Drywalls: Built with double-layer gypsum boards on heavy metal studs with dense mineral wool insulation (rockwool) inside the cavity. Essential for secure meeting rooms and offices.

Our Execution Process

We make sure each office fitout is completed quickly to avoid disrupting your business operations. Our process includes:

  1. Consultation & Space Planning: We study your workflow and design a detailed floor plan to maximize traffic efficiency and desk counts.
  2. Material Sourcing & Prep: We source heavy-duty profiles and safety glass certified for commercial building codes in Kenya.
  3. Framing & Glazing: Frames are erected, anchored to floor/ceilings, and safety glass is fitted with compression gaskets.
  4. Frosting & Branding: We apply custom decorative frosted window film (solid, striped, or featuring your corporate logo) for additional privacy.

Office Partitioning Cost in Nairobi

Frameless glass partitioning typically starts from KES 8,000 to KES 12,000 per square meter depending on glass thickness and door hardware. Aluminium profile systems are generally more economical, starting at KES 5,500 per square meter. Drywall installations with insulation run between KES 3,500 to KES 5,000 per square meter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do glass office partitions block sound?

A: Yes. Standard 10mm tempered glass provides significant noise reduction (around 32-35 dB), which is sufficient for normal office speech privacy. For absolute soundproofing, we install double-glazed glass partitions.

Q: Can glass partitions be dismantled and moved?

A: Yes. Unlike brick or drywall, aluminium and glass partition structures can be carefully dismantled and relocated to a new office block if your business decides to move premises.

Q: What safety standards do you use for office glass?

A: We exclusively use high-strength tempered safety glass or laminated safety glass. Tempered glass is up to 5 times stronger than normal glass and crumbles into small blunt pieces if broken, reducing injury risk.
