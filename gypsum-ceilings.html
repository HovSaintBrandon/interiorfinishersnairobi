Gypsum Ceiling Installation in Nairobi

Elevate your interiors with modern, perfectly finished gypsum ceilings and bulkheads tailored to your space.

Why Choose Gypsum Ceilings?

Gypsum ceilings (also known as false ceilings) are the gold standard for modern interior finishing in Kenya. They offer a sleek, seamless look that hides wiring and imperfections while providing excellent acoustic and thermal insulation.

Our Styling Options

  • Modern Bulkheads: Define separate areas in open-plan spaces (like dining vs. living) without walls.
  • Hidden Lighting (Cove Lighting): Create ambiance with LED strips tucked into ceiling recesses.
  • Geometric Shapes: Custom circles, squares, or waves to add artistic flair to your ceiling.

Benefits

Fire Resistance: Gypsum is naturally fire-resistant, adding safety to your home.
Sound Insulation: Reduces echo and noise transfer between floors.
Cooling: Helps maintain cooler indoor temperatures in Nairobi's warm climate.
