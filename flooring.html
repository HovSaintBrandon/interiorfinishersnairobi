Premium Flooring Solutions in Nairobi

Transform your steps with durable, high-end hardwood, epoxy, SPC, and laminate flooring options.

Bespoke Residential & Commercial Flooring Installations

The right flooring defines the mood, acoustic properties, and durability of your interior. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we deliver precision floor installations using premium materials that withstand foot traffic, resist moisture, and complement your overall design language.

Whether you want a high-gloss seamless epoxy floor for a modern office lobby in Westlands, warm and cozy hardwood parquet for a living room in Karen, or waterproof SPC tiles for a high-traffic rental apartment in Kilimani, we offer complete site evaluation, subfloor preparation, laying, and sealing services.

Our Flooring Material Specialties

  • Solid Wood & Parquet: Authentic mahogany, teak, or oak wood blocks laid in herringbone, mosaic, or running patterns, sanded and finished with polyurethane sealant.
  • Epoxy Resin Floors: Ultra-durable, jointless, and highly hygienic self-leveling floors. Available in solid colors, metallic patterns, and 3D floor finishes.
  • SPC (Stone Plastic Composite): The latest generation of rigid vinyl planks. Fully waterproof, scratch-proof, and featuring an underlayment that provides excellent foot-sound insulation.
  • Laminate Planks: Cost-effective HDF core boards with a wood-look photo layer and highly durable wear layer, ideal for bedrooms and playrooms.

Our Subfloor Preparation & Laying Process

A beautiful floor is 90% preparation. We execute a rigorous multi-step installation process to prevent moisture bubbling or plank separation:

  1. Moisture Testing & Leveling: We measure the moisture content of the concrete screed. Any humps are ground down, and hollows are filled with self-leveling compound to ensure a perfectly flat surface.
  2. Damp-Proof Membrane (DPM): We apply a epoxy-based liquid damp-proof membrane to prevent subfloor rising moisture from reaching wood or laminate.
  3. Acoustic Underlayment: For SPC and laminate, we lay a high-density foam or cork underlay to cushion the floor and reduce sound impact.
  4. Precision Cutting & Laying: We install planks or pour epoxy resin under controlled temperature conditions. Planks are fitted with expansion gaps around the perimeter.
  5. Skirting & Profile Trims: We finish the perimeter using solid wood or PVC skirting boards, transition strips, and T-moldings.

Premium solid hardwood parquet ranges between KES 5,000 to KES 8,500 per square meter depending on the timber type and finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between SPC and Laminate flooring?

A: SPC flooring has a stone-plastic composite core which is 100% waterproof and highly dent-resistant. Laminate has a wood-fiber HDF core which can swell if exposed to standing water. SPC is better for kitchens and dining areas.

Q: Can epoxy flooring be installed on damaged concrete floors?

A: Yes, but the concrete must be thoroughly ground, patched, and primed first. Any structural cracks must be filled with epoxy mortar to prevent reflection cracks appearing on the new finish.

Q: How long does solid wood parquet flooring last?

A: With proper care, solid wood parquet can last for over 50 years. It can be sanded and re-varnished every 10-15 years to look brand new again.
