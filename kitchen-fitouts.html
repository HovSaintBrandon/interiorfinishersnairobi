Custom Kitchen Fitouts in Nairobi

Design a kitchen that is both highly functional and visually breathtaking, custom-made for your home.

Luxury Kitchen Cabinets & Smart Joinery Solutions

The kitchen is the heart of every home. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we believe a kitchen should be as efficient to cook in as it is elegant to entertain in. We specialize in bespoke kitchen fitouts, designing custom cabinetry layouts that optimize storage, traffic flow, and food preparation ergonomics.

From contemporary handleless open-plan kitchens in Kilimani apartments to classic wood-grain shaker kitchens in Runda mansions, we work with you from 3D modeling to physical hand-over. We integrate high-quality hardware to ensure drawers and doors glide effortlessly for years to come.

Custom Cabinetry & Material Options

  • Premium Board Finishes: High-density fiberboard (HDF) and moisture-resistant MDF clad in high-gloss acrylic, matte laminates, or natural wood veneers.
  • Luxury Countertops: We supply and install durable quartz stone, natural granite, and solid acrylic surfaces that are scratch, heat, and stain-resistant.
  • Soft-Close Hardware: We exclusively use original Blum soft-close hinges, pull-out pantry baskets, and corner carousel organizers for modern accessibility.

Our Execution Process

Our professional kitchen design process ensures structural accuracy and beauty:

  1. Space Inspection & Measurement: We perform precise laser measurements of walls, plumbing inlets, power points, and gas vents.
  2. 3D Design Render: We build a 3D digital model of your kitchen layout, allowing you to preview cabinet configurations, color schemes, and appliance placements.
  3. Offsite Fabrication: Cabinet carcasses are machined, edge-banded, and pre-assembled in our workshop to guarantee structural squareness.
  4. Onsite Installation: Our technicians mount the cabinets, align the doors, install the countertops, cut openings for sinks and hobs, and fit accessories.

Kitchen Fitout Cost in Nairobi

A custom kitchen fitout in Kenya typically ranges from KES 250,000 to KES 850,000+ depending on kitchen linear size, chosen cabinet door finish (acrylic vs. laminate), countertop stone (granite vs. imported quartz), and hardware pull-out organizer accessories. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best board for kitchen cabinet framing?

A: We recommend Moisture-Resistant MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) or high-grade plywood. Standard particle boards are susceptible to swelling in damp kitchen conditions, so we avoid them.

Q: How long does a kitchen renovation take?

A: The offsite cabinet fabrication takes about 10 to 14 working days. The onsite demolition, cabinet installation, stone countertop polishing, and appliance mounting takes 4 to 7 working days.

Q: Can you install built-in kitchen appliances?

A: Yes. We coordinate with appliance retailers to ensure precise cavity dimensions for built-in ovens, microwaves, dishwashers, double-door refrigerators, and chimneys.
