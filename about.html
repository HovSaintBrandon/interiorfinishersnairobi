Who We Are

Leading Interior Finishing Contractors in Nairobi

Founded on the principles of integrity, transparency, and absolute craftsmanship, InteriorFinishes Nairobi has grown from a local finishing team to a full-service commercial and residential interior contracting company. Based in Parklands, we serve corporate offices, retail spaces, and luxury homes throughout Nairobi, including Karen, Runda, Westlands, and Kilimani.

Our capability spans design rendering, custom workshop joinery fabrication, ceiling construction, wall paneling, and advanced smart systems integration. By controlling our supply chains and employing permanent, specialized technical teams, we guarantee timelines and budget accuracy for every client.
