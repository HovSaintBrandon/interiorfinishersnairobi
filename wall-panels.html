Modern Wall Panels & Finishes in Nairobi

Make a statement with our range of decorative wall panels, fluted cladding, and bespoke finishes.

Redefine Your Walls

Gone are the days of plain walls. Wall panels add texture, depth, and luxury to any room. Whether it's a TV feature wall in your living room or a branded reception wall for your office, we have the perfect solution.

Our Finish Options

  • Fluted Panels (WPC): Popular reliable wood-plastic composite panels that are water-proof and termite-proof. Great for TV units.
  • 3D Wall Cladding: Geometric patterns that pop out to create a stunning visual interest.
  • Textured Paints: High-quality decorative paints including silk, stucco, and metallic finishes.

Applications

TV Backgrounds: Integrate panels with floating shelves for a modern media center.
Bedroom Headboards: Create a cozy, hotel-like vibe in your master bedroom.
Office Receptions: Impress clients the moment they walk in.
