Modern Wall Panels & Finishes in Nairobi

Make a statement with our range of decorative wall panels, fluted cladding, and bespoke finishes.

Elevate Interiors with Premium Wall Panels & Cladding

Plain walls are a thing of the past. Modern interior styling utilizes tactile depth, texture, and natural grain to make walls a focus point. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we supply and install top-grade decorative wall panels that add instant luxury, comfort, and architectural character to your home or commercial office.

Whether you are building a custom TV wall panel set with integrated LED backlighting in Kilimani, introducing a fluted WPC accent wall in a Karen villa, or designing an impressive corporate logo wall in Westlands, our skilled craftsmen deliver a seamless, clean installation that lasts.

Our Wall Cladding & Panel Options

  • Fluted WPC Panels: Made from wood-plastic composites, these panels offer the look of natural timber slats with the added durability of composites. They are water, moisture, and insect-proof.
  • 3D Geometric Cladding: Textured PVC or plaster panels that create dynamic light and shadow play. Excellent for commercial lobbies and creative workspaces.
  • Natural Wood Veneers & MDF: Customized CNC routing panels finished with premium varnishes or metallic skimming for warm, corporate meeting spaces.

Our Installation Process

We make sure each wall cladding project has a perfectly flat and secure installation:

  1. Wall Assessment: We test the wall for moisture and flatness. Any cracks or dampness are sealed and leveled.
  2. Batten Grid Setup: For uneven walls or TV cables, we install a timber or metal batten system to create a flat substrate and hide wiring.
  3. Adhesive & Clip Fastening: Panels are mounted using industrial-strength polyurethane adhesive (liquid nails) and secured with stainless steel clips to prevent shifting.
  4. Trimming & LED Fitting: We complete the look with matching L-angles, edge bands, and warm LED strip lighting recessed into the panel tracks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are WPC fluted panels waterproof?

A: Yes, WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) is fully waterproof and termite-resistant, making it safe for dining walls, corridors, and semi-outdoor verandas.

Q: Can I hang a heavy TV directly on fluted panels?

A: No. Heavy items should be anchored through the panels into the brick wall behind or fixed onto the internal wooden backing grid that we build during installation.

Q: Do you offer custom color painting for wall panels?

A: WPC panels come in pre-finished colors (like walnut, charcoal, teak, and oak) and cannot be easily painted. For custom colors, we install paintable MDF or gypsum 3D wall panels.
