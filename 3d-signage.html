3D Signage & Branding in Nairobi

Capture customer attention with laser-cut acrylic letters, illuminated channel logos, and professional interior wall branding.

Custom Laser-Cut Acrylic, Metal & LED Signage

First impressions are critical for corporate office spaces and retail shops alike. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we fabricate high-end 3D signage and office reception branding that projects authority, high quality, and modern sophistication. We turn vector files into physical logos that catch light and demand attention.

From backlit acrylic logos on timber slatted reception walls in Westlands to outdoor stainless steel channel letters on commercial complexes in Kilimani, we offer complete graphic conversion, laser CNC cutting, LED assembly, and masonry installation. Let us help your company stand out in the crowded Nairobi marketplace.

Signage Types & Fabrication Options

  • Illuminated Acrylic Letters: 3D letters fabricated from colorful cast acrylic with internal Samsung LED modules. The faces glow brightly, offering maximum night visibility for retail shops.
  • Halo-Lit Metal Letters: Premium channel letters cut from mirror stainless steel, brass, or powder-coated aluminium. LEDs project light backward onto the wall, creating a clean "halo" glow.
  • Non-Illuminated Solid Letters: CNC routed solid acrylic (5mm, 10mm) or dense foam letters mounted on double-sided tape and silicone adhesive, perfect for budget-friendly indoor reception walls.
  • Custom Lightboxes: Fabricated steel or aluminium profiles with stretched flexface or opal acrylic faces, vinyl printed and brightly backlit for storefront pylons.

Our Fabrication & Installation Roadmap

We make sure each sign has a clean finish, correct electrical load calculations, and safe wall anchorage:

  1. Vector File Validation: We scale your corporate vector logo (.eps, .cdr, or .pdf) to the planned wall dimensions and provide 3D mockups.
  2. CNC Laser Cutting: Faces and side returns are cut with precision laser machines to ensure smooth edges and curves.
  3. LED & Wiring Assembly: IP67 waterproof LED modules are hot-glued inside the letters, and all electrical connections are run to a hidden 12V MeanWell power converter.
  4. Template Installation: We tape a paper template to your wall to mark screw locations, drill anchors, run wires behind the wall or through conduits, and lock letters into place.

3D Signage Pricing in Nairobi

Indoor corporate logo walls (acrylic letters on reception drywall/board) typically range from KES 25,000 to KES 55,000. Outdoor illuminated LED channel letter signage ranges from KES 3,500 to KES 7,500 per letter depending on height (e.g. 30cm vs 60cm letter height) and materials used. Standard double-sided projection lightboxes cost between KES 15,000 and KES 35,000.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your outdoor 3D signs waterproof and rustproof?

A: Yes. We use marine-grade stainless steel (Grade 304 or 316), powder-coated rustproof aluminium, and cast acrylic panels. All LED modules and power supplies have an IP67 waterproof rating to resist Nairobi rains.

Q: How long do LEDs in 3D signage last?

A: We exclusively use high-efficiency commercial LED chips with a lifespan rating of 50,000 hours. If run for 10 hours every night, the sign will shine brightly for over 13 years before needing replacement.

Q: Can you install corporate branding graphics on glass walls?

A: Yes. We supply and plot frosted vinyl films, contour-cut vinyl graphics, and colorful one-way vision stickers directly onto glass office doors and boardrooms.
