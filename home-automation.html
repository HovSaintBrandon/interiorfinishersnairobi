Smart Home Automation in Nairobi

Connect your home. Experience convenience, energy savings, and ultimate control from your phone or voice.

Luxury Smart Home Systems & Custom Audio-Visual Layouts

Modern luxury is defined by seamless comfort and control. At InteriorFinishes Nairobi, we engineer advanced smart home automation systems that integrate lighting, motorized window shades, HVAC, and multi-room audio into a single, intuitive interface. Say goodbye to clutter and hello to one-touch scene control.

From fully automated smart villas in Karen to modern connected penthouses in Kilimani and media room installations in Runda, we work directly with architects and interior designers to pre-wire or retrofit smart technology discreetly. Control your home via sleek touch panels, mobile apps, or simple voice commands.

Smart Solutions We Integrate

  • Smart Lighting Control: Replace standard light switches with elegant smart dimmers. Create automated schedules (like "Welcome Home" or "Night Mode") that save energy and set the perfect mood.
  • Motorized Curtain Tracks: Automated drape and blind systems that open and close based on sunrise, temperature limits, or app touch. Highly recommended for tall double-height windows.
  • Multi-Room Audio & Theater: Flush-mount ceiling and outdoor garden speakers connected to centralized smart amplifiers, letting you stream music in any zone.
  • Smart Security Integration: Smart biometric door locks, video intercoms, CCTV, and water leak detectors linked to push notifications for absolute peace of mind.

Our Consultation & Installation Protocol

To guarantee zero connectivity drops and a neat finish, we follow a rigorous tech integration roadmap:

  1. Tech Infrastructure Design: We assess your floor plan and map out Wi-Fi/mesh router hubs, cable drops, and power outlets for smart hubs.
  2. Discreet Pre-Wiring: During construction or plastering, we pull low-voltage CAT6 cables, speaker wires, and motorized curtain power conduits.
  3. Hardware Mounting & Programming: We mount touchscreens, wire smart breakers in your distribution board, and program device links.
  4. Client Handover & App Setup: We connect the system to your personal smartphone and tablet, setup voice commands, and train you on customizing lighting scenes.

Smart Home Automation Cost in Nairobi

A basic smart home retrofitting package (covering smart lighting and voice setup for a living and dining room) starts from KES 150,000 to KES 300,000. Complete high-end wired automation systems (covering lighting, motorized curtains, security, and multi-room audio for a 4-bedroom villa) typically range from KES 800,000 to KES 2,500,000+ depending on system brands (Lutron, Control4, Tuya, or Sonoff).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can home automation be installed in a completed house?

A: Yes. For existing finished houses, we deploy wireless mesh protocols (like Zigbee or Z-Wave) that use smart switches and modules fitting right inside standard electrical wall boxes, requiring zero wall breaking.

Q: What happens to my smart home lights if the Wi-Fi or power goes out?

A: Smart switches still function as standard manual wall buttons if the internet goes down. In the event of a power blackout, we install backup UPS batteries to keep the smart home controller and security hubs running.

Q: Which control brands do you recommend?

A: For enterprise-level wired reliability, we recommend Control4, Crestron, or KNX. For budget-friendly flexible wireless systems, we recommend Sonoff, Tuya, and smart hubs integrated via Apple HomeKit, Google Home, or Amazon Alexa.
